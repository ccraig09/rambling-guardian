# Speech Coaching App Competitor Analysis (2026-04-05)

> Research for Rambling Guardian Phase C+ planning. Covers speech coaching apps,
> exercise types, progression models, and gamification patterns.

## 1. Speeko AI (Primary Inspiration)

**Overview:** AI speech coach for public speaking. iOS + Mac. $9.99/month ($49.99/year).

**Core Features:**
- Real-time speech analysis: pace, pitch variation, volume, filler words, word choice, strategic pauses
- Record directly or analyze calls from Zoom, Slack, Teams, Google Meet
- Recordings stored locally for privacy
- Transcript analysis for fillers, conciseness, jargon, non-inclusive expressions

**Exercises:**
- Bite-sized (2 minutes/day)
- Daily vocal warm-ups for muscle memory
- Interview prep with timed sessions
- Interactive courses: storytelling, impromptu speaking, managing nerves, online presentations
- Content from Roger Love (voice coach)
- New content added regularly

**Free Tier ("Vocal Delivery"):** Breathing, projection, pacing, filler reduction, monotone avoidance

**Premium Modules:** Great Orators, impromptu speaking, audience engagement, storytelling, nervousness, body language

**Progression:** Adaptive lessons based on prior performance, post-session report cards, personalized recommendations, data-driven metrics with graphs

**Weaknesses:** Light on gamification (no XP/streaks/badges), stability issues, data-heavy presentation overwhelms beginners, iOS/Mac only

## 2. Direct Competitors

### Orai — iOS + Android, $12.99/month
- AI feedback on clarity, pace, energy, filler words, facial expressions, conciseness, confidence
- **Gamified lessons with badges, streaks, progress charts** (strongest gamification)
- Practice modes: impromptu, storytelling, persuasive speaking, interviews
- Scorecards and history with growth visualization
- Multi-accent support

### Yoodli — Browser-based, endorsed by Toastmasters
- Filler word tracking, pacing, body language via webcam
- Exercises: "Talking Without Filler Words", Analogies Challenge, Impromptu Drills
- **Leaderboard gamification** ("Who can lower filler count the most?")
- Zoom/Teams/Meet integration for live meeting analysis
- Customizable roleplay scenarios
- Instant transcripts

### Poised — Desktop (meetings-focused)
- Real-time feedback during live virtual meetings
- Tracks: tone, pace, fillers, eye contact, empathy, confidence, clarity
- **"Poised Score" out of 100** per meeting
- Live Speaker Notes (auto-check items as covered)
- Auto-generated meeting summaries and action items
- Industry benchmarking
- **Built on Deepgram** (relevant to our Phase D stack)

## 3. Pronunciation & Accent Training

### BoldVoice — iOS
- Hollywood accent coaches in video lessons
- Mouth placement diagrams, tongue placement guides, IPA-based
- Color-coded AI pronunciation feedback at phoneme level
- Minimal pair drills (bat/pat, sip/zip)
- Daily structure: Challenge Words + Interactive Video Lessons
- Sound Library (vowel/consonant guides)
- AI Chat for scenario practice
- Progression: foundational sounds → stress → intonation → connected speech
- **Daily targets and streaks**

### ELSA Speak — iOS + Android
- 6,000+ exercises across 40+ topics
- Phoneme-level pronunciation analysis
- Word stress, intonation, rhythm training
- CEFR-level predictions (A1 to C1)
- **Gamification: scores, badges, streaks, green progress bars**
- Initial assessment creates customized curriculum
- Detailed performance reports

## 5. Exercise Categories

### Vocal Warmups
- **Jaw:** Massage jaw hinge muscles, open-close repetitions (5-10 reps), facial massage
- **Lip trills:** Vibrating lips while vocalizing, pitch range building
- **Humming:** Relax vocal cords, resonance building
- **Sirens:** Sliding pitch up and down smoothly
- **"Ma, Pa, Ta" drills:** Articulation calisthenics for mouth, lips, tongue
- **Sustained vowels:** Holding "ah", "ee", "oo" for 3-5 seconds each

### Breathing
- **Diaphragmatic:** Book-on-stomach technique
- **Counted:** Inhale 4, hold 4, exhale 4, progressively increase
- **Projection:** Controlled exhalation for sustained vocalization
- **Pre-speech calming:** Guided meditation-style

### Articulation Drills
- **Tongue twisters:** Progressive difficulty, speed and clarity targets
- **Minimal pairs:** bat/pat, sip/zip — distinguishing similar sounds
- **Exaggerated vowels:** IPA vowel cycling with lip/tongue monitoring
- **Voiced vs voiceless tap test:** Fingers on throat to feel vibration
- **Tongue position drills:** Specific placements for /r/, /l/, /th/
- **Stress marking:** Written words with stressed syllables exaggerated
- **Word linking:** Connected speech drills ("I'm gonna" instead of "I'm going to")

### Speech Practice
- **Impromptu speaking:** Random topics with time limits
- **Storytelling:** Structured narrative delivery
- **Presentation simulation:** Timed practice with prompts
- **Interview prep:** Common questions with timed responses
- **Shadowing:** Repeat native speakers, matching rhythm/intonation
- **Self-recording playback:** Record, replay, compare to reference
- **Roleplay scenarios:** Conversational practice (interviews, meetings, social)

### Filler Word Reduction
- **Detection & counting:** Real-time tracking of "um", "uh", "like", "so", "actually"
- **Custom word tracking:** User-defined words to monitor
- **Pause training:** Replace fillers with intentional pauses
- **Reduction challenges:** Timed speaking with filler count targets

## 6. Progression Models

| Model | Used By | How It Works |
|-------|---------|-------------|
| **Assessment-first** | ELSA, BoldVoice | Initial diagnostic → custom curriculum targeting weaknesses |
| **Adaptive sequencing** | Speeko | Each lesson adapts based on prior performance |
| **Analytics dashboard** | Orai, Poised, Yoodli | Scorecards, historical charts, benchmarking |
| **Skill tree / modules** | Speeko, ELSA | Sequential unlocking based on mastery |
| **Daily targets** | BoldVoice | 2 daily activities (Challenge Words + Video Lesson) |

---

## 7. Gamification Patterns

| Pattern | Used By | Notes |
|---------|---------|-------|
| **Streaks** | Orai, ELSA, BoldVoice | Consecutive daily practice. Loss aversion psychology. |
| **Badges** | Orai, ELSA | Milestone + mastery + challenge completion |
| **Progress bars** | ELSA, BoldVoice | Green fill as skills mastered. Color-coded feedback. |
| **Leaderboards** | Yoodli | Competitive filler reduction. Social accountability. |
| **Scoring** | Poised (0-100), Orai, BoldVoice | Per-session scorecards, pronunciation accuracy % |
| **Daily challenges** | Speeko (2min), BoldVoice (2 activities), Yoodli (weekly) | Micro-goals for consistency |

**Not yet common in speech coaching (opportunity):**
- XP/currency systems (Duolingo-style)
- Social features / friend competitions
- Reward shops / unlockable cosmetics
- Story-driven progression
